Admission Process

  1. Step 1: Fill out the online application form.
  2. Step 2: Submit required documents (e.g., ID, prior transcripts).
  3. Step 3: Attend an online or in-person interview.
  4. Step 4: Receive your admission decision via email.
  5. Step 5: Complete the registration by paying fees and attending orientation.
